About CRCHUB

CRCHUB Inc. provides Academic Institutions, turn-key Cloud Based Web Application
to effectively manage their Corporate Resource Center (Placement Cell).
Using cutting edge technologies, this software makes it very easy and convenient to manage an interactive
Job center, Career Events and Bulletin Board.

Why CRCHUB?

The possibility and challenges for educational institutions have changed globally in the recent decade. The shifting paradigm places burden on the institutions to engage students in a comprehensive career building opportunity from the onset so they not only have an enriching learning experience but succeed once in the real world. To cater to this need most institutes now have a career development department which supports and encourages students to excel, learn and achieve.
The Indian Placement Reporting Standards (IPRS) aims for the standardization of Placement Reports to make them more transparent and easy to understand, more comparable and reliable for making decisions. This has become a challenging task for most of the Academic Institutions in India. That is where we come to help you overcome the challenges faced by the placement cell of your institute by achieving better efficiency and reporting through CRCHUB.

CHALLENGES IN CREATING A GREAT PLACEMENT CELL

  • Institutes rely on placement officers and physical documents to manage their placement statistics and spend an inordinate amount of time creating, revising and managing that data.


  • Retrieving information requires manual scanning and archiving of the data is complex and costly.

  • Physical documents have short shelf life and are difficult to trace.

  • Accessibility of the physical documents from various sources

  • Compiling placement reports

Benefits

Top Management . HOD’S Staff Placement cell . Students . Employers

Benefits for Top Management:

:Makes information more accessible at one click: which was considered too tedious and time consuming to obtain
Fosters collaborative functioning: by enabling reports to be shared with all members of the top management without communicating using spread sheets
Reduces the cost of generating reports: An all purpose Reports Section which provides detailed profiles and performance of Students, Alumni and Employers with no extra effort or cost.

Benefits for HOD’S/ Staff/Placement cell:

: Prepares placement staff to be able to respond to performance related queries and enabling them to identify and take action on performance improvement opportunities;
Increases effectiveness by revealing gap areas and trends earlier, enabling action to be taken well in advance.
Minimises the burden of reporting
Placement Performance Tracking: The Training and Placement Officers (TPO) can view performance in placement with three different contexts – Students& Alumni, Job Postings and Employers. For each of these the TPO knows the latest status real time.

Benefits for Students :

: Create and Manage their own profile
Job Application management: Need not send their profiles again and again for the job posted.
The Career Resource & Industry Updates provide career guidance information, resume & interview preparation guides
Information about Online Psychometric tests, Quizzes, employability Tests provided.
Resume Building: Ability to add part time and full time job experiences to the profile.

Benefits for Employers :

: Manage Profile: Employers interested in hiring from the college can sign up on the website
Post Jobs: They can post their requirement directly. The TPO on receipt of the Job Post request from employer, approve it and post it on the online notice board of the college.
Manage Applications: Employers can view performance of their Job Postings and Events for number of applicants and also view the information of students who have applied for the openings.
